About Our Online Shopping Project

Welcome to our Online Shopping Blog! Our project aims to provide a seamless and enjoyable shopping experience for our users. We offer a wide range of products and ensure that our customers have access to the best deals and offers.

Functional Requirements

  • Home: Overview of the online shopping platform.
  • Products: Detailed list of available products with descriptions and prices.
  • Shopping Cart: Users can add, view, and modify items in their cart.
  • Contact: Contact information for customer support.

HTML Tags Used

  • <header>: Defines the header section of the page.
  • <nav>: Contains navigation links for easy access to different sections.
  • <section>: Represents a distinct section of content.
  • <main>: Encloses the main content of the page.
  • <footer>: Defines the footer section with contact information and copyright notice.
  • <h1>, <h2>, <h3>: Heading tags for various levels of headings.
  • <p>: Paragraph tag to define blocks of text.
  • <a>: Anchor tag for creating hyperlinks.
  • <ul>, <li>: Unordered list and list item tags for creating lists.
  • <img>: Image tag for displaying images.
  • <button>: Button tag for creating clickable buttons.
  • <address>: Tag for defining contact information.
  • <style>: Tag for defining CSS styles directly within the HTML document.
